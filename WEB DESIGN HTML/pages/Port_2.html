DATA AND INFORMATION

WHAT IS DATA?


Data is raw information, such as numbers, text, or measurements, that can be collected, analyzed, and used to draw conclusions or make decisions. When data is given context, it becomes information, which can then lead to knowledge.

WHAT IS INFORMATION


Information is knowledge, facts, or data communicated or received about a particular subject, event, or process. It can be conveyed through various means like written words, pictures, or spoken language, and it helps resolve uncertainty, answer questions, and guide decision-making.

TYPES OF SUPPORT SYSTEMS




- TPS (TRANSACTION PROCESSING SYSTEMS): DAILY OPERATIONS.
- MIS (MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS): REPORTS & SUMMARIES.
- DSS (DECISION SUPPORT SYSTEMS): “WHAT-IF” SCENARIOS, ANALYSIS.
- ESS/EIS (EXECUTIVE SUPPORT SYSTEMS): DASHBOARDS FOR EXECUTIVES.
- EXPERT SYSTEMS & KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS: KNOWLEDGE-BASED REASONING.
