COMPUTER HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE IN COMMUNITY INSTITUTIONS

INTRODUCTION


Computers have become an important part of our daily life. Both hardware (the physical parts) and software (the programs and applications) are used in different institutions to make work faster and easier. In this portfolio, I will discuss how computer hardware and software are used in various community institutions.

SCHOOLS

in Schools:hardware used are Computers, projectors, printers, and tablets. And those hardware is equipped with software such as, Learning management systems (LMS) like Google Classroom, Microsoft Office, and educational apps. Teachers use computers to prepare lessons, grade students, and communicate with parents. Students use computers for research and online learning.

HOSPITALS

In Hospitals:Computers, scanners, MRI machines, and monitors. (hardware) mostly for doing tests and management. Hospital management software, electronic medical records (EMR), and diagnostic software (software). Doctors and nurses use computers to keep patient records, schedule appointments, and analyze test results.

GOVERNMENT OFFICES

In Local Govenrment Officess: >Workers use Computers, printers, scanners, and servers with Document management systems, online service platforms, and and accounting software. Local offices use computers to manage public records, issue certificates, and collect payments efficiently.

BUSINESS

In Business: Computers, POS systems, and networking equipment are used as the hardware while Accounting software, customer management systems, productivity tools.is used as the softwareBusinesses use computers for accounting, inventory, sales tracking, and communication with clients.
